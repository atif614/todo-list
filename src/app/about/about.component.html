ToDo List App is a kind of app that generally used to maintain our day-to-day tasks or list everything that we have to do, with the most important tasks at the top of the list, and the least important tasks at the bottom. It is helpful in planning our daily schedules. We can add more tasks at any time and delete a task that is completed.

This Todo App has a three section .First section will have the list of todo and second one is the InProgress part and third one is the completed part. When a user will add his task then it will be added to todo list, when his task is in progress then it can be dragged to the InProgress part and when the task of the user is completed then finally he will drag it to the Completed part.

This TodoList App works in any web browsers such as Chrome, Firefox, Internet Explorer, Safari, Opera.
